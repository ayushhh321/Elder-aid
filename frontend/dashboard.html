Assistance Request T&C


1. Upon submitting an assistance request, volunteers will be notified and can choose to accept or decline your request.


2. Volunteers have the right to accept or reject any assistance request based on their availability.


3. If a volunteer accepts your request, you will be redirected to a secure, encrypted messaging and calling system for further communication.


4. It may take up to 5 hours to match you with a suitable volunteer. We appreciate your patience.


5. Use "Submit Assistance Request" only when Urgency Needed.
