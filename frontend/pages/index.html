Connecting Elderly With Caring Volunteers

Find reliable assistance for daily tasks, companionship, and healthcare needs.

How It Works

Create Profile

Sign up and create your profile as an elderly person or a volunteer.

Request Assistance

Submit requests for the type of help you need or browse available tasks.

Get Matched

Our system matches elderly users with suitable volunteers based on needs and skills.

Receive Support

Connect with your matched volunteer and receive the assistance you need.

Our Services

Grocery Shopping

Get help with grocery shopping and delivery to your doorstep.

Transportation

Assistance with transportation to appointments, errands, or social activities.

Companionship

Enjoy conversation, games, reading, or simply having someone to talk to.

Healthcare Support

Medication reminders, doctor appointment assistance, and health monitoring.
