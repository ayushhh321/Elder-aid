Privacy Policy

At Elderly Assistance Portal, we're committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information remains secure. This document outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

To provide our service connecting elderly individuals with volunteers, we collect various types of information:

For Elderly Users:

  • Personal Information: Name, age, contact details, and address
  • Health Information: Health conditions, specific needs, and medical history when voluntarily provided
  • Service Requests: Details about assistance needed, preferred times, locations, and budgets
  • Communication Content: Messages and call records between you and volunteers
  • Payment Information: Financial data related to compensating volunteers

For Volunteers:

  • Personal Information: Name, age, contact details, and location
  • Skills & Services: Information about services you can provide
  • Availability: Your schedule and availability to provide assistance
  • Background Check Information: Verification details to ensure platform safety
  • Payment Details: Information needed to receive compensation for services provided
  • Service Rates: Your payment rates for different types of assistance

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device & Usage Information: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and usage patterns
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address or precise location with your permission
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: Information stored on your device to enhance your experience

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To match elderly users with appropriate volunteers based on needs, location, and availability
  • Profile Creation: To establish and maintain user accounts and profiles
  • Communication: To facilitate messaging and calls between elderly users and volunteers
  • Payment Processing: To handle transactions between elderly users and volunteers
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and enhance our platform's functionality
  • Safety & Security: To verify user identities and maintain a secure environment
  • Personalization: To tailor our services to your preferences and needs
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Information Sharing

We share information with the following parties:

  • Between Users: Relevant information is shared between elderly users and volunteers to facilitate assistance services
  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our platform, process payments, and provide customer support
  • Background Check Services: For volunteer verification purposes
  • Family Members: With the elderly user's explicit consent, information may be shared with designated family members
  • Healthcare Providers: When voluntarily provided by elderly users and with their consent
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security Measures

We implement various security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data transmission is protected using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Secure Infrastructure: Regular security assessments and updates to our systems
  • Access Controls: Strict limitations on who can access user information within our organization
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Staff Training: Comprehensive security training for all personnel with access to user data
  • Incident Response: Procedures to quickly address any potential security breaches

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to protect your personal information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Health Information Privacy

We understand the sensitive nature of health information and take additional precautions:

  • Consent-Based: Health information is only collected with explicit consent
  • Limited Access: Only authorized individuals can access health-related data
  • Enhanced Security: Additional security measures are applied to health information
  • Controlled Sharing: Health information is only shared with designated volunteers, healthcare providers, or family members with your permission
  • Deletion Protocols: Special procedures for the deletion of health data upon request

Our practices regarding health information comply with applicable healthcare privacy regulations.

Payment Security

To ensure the security of payment transactions between elderly users and volunteers:

  • PCI Compliance: Our payment processing systems adhere to Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards
  • Third-Party Processors: We use established payment processors with robust security measures
  • Limited Retention: Full payment details are not stored on our servers
  • Transaction Monitoring: Systems to detect and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • Secure Payments: All payment transactions are encrypted end-to-end

Your Rights & Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information in certain circumstances
  • Restriction: You can request that we restrict the processing of your information
  • Data Portability: You can request to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Communication Preferences: You can opt out of receiving promotional communications
  • Device Permissions: You can control permissions such as location access through your device settings
  • Cookie Management: You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@elderlyassistance.org.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on our website
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this policy
  • Notify you through the platform or via email for significant changes

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
